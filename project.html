How to Practice Chords

Practice moving between these chords on the keyboard. Keep your hand in the same shape. We like to call this fingering shape (1-3-5) ‚Äúthe Claw.‚Äù

A great way to practice is by playing four C chords in a steady rhythm, followed by four F chords and so on. You can gradually progress your way through the sequence and speed up until your muscle memory starts to kick in.

Experiment with the order of things. These four chords are going to sound great no matter how you choose to play them, so switch things up and see what you can create.
